What is SASR?
SASR (semi-automatic Systematic Review) is a tool that is based on data processing techniques to obtain relationships and statistics from its bibliography of Mendeley, Zootero, JABref, and others.
How to use SASR?
1. Upload a .bib or .ris file with the "Upload File" button
2. Press the different buttons to create visualizations and statistics from your uploaded file
What can be obtained with SASR?
Co-authorship network button Relationships between authors and papers (click on an author to go to your Google Scholar).
Reports and statistics button Articles and authors per year, map of keywords, map of principal authors, type of document (article, book, conference, and others.), finally, each article with its download link.
